The Analysis

Why Users Bounce: The "Value Gap"

1. Trust & Risk

User Need: "Is my business safe?"

Site Problem: No Case Studies. Shallow content on protection.

Impact: Low Trust.

2. Utility & Cost

User Need: "Can I afford this?"

Site Problem: "Enquire Now" button instead of a Calculator.

Impact: High Friction.

3. Clarity

User Need: "Is this for me?"

Site Problem: Irrelevant content (Restaurants next to Laws).

Impact: Paralysis.

The Result: The user leaves to find a simpler solution (UK LLC).

The Audience Gap

We think the user knows:

  • "I want DIFC specifically."
  • "I know what English Common Law is."
  • "I am ready to enquire."

Reality: "The Learner" (B2B)

The user actually asks:

  • "Can I afford Dubai?"

  • "Why not just open a £300 UK LLC?"

The disconnect: We are selling "Prestige" to users who are buying "Safety & Utility".

UX & Conversion

Improving User Retention

The Problem: "Low Motivation"

  • ⚠ Friction: Users see "Enquire" and expect a sales call.
  • ⚠ Generic CTA: "Enquire Now" is visible but not compelling.
  • Insight: Users want utility (costs), not a meeting.

The Solution: "Value-Led CTA"

  • Change The Ask: Swap "Enquire" for "Get Cost Estimate".
  • Reduce Friction: Simplify form to 3 essential fields.
  • Benefit-Driven H1s: "Launch Your Firm in Dubai's #1 Hub".

"Stop selling 'Memberships'. Start solving Business Problems."
